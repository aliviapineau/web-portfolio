Type semiconductor competitor Nvidia into the Wall Street Truth Detector, and the warning lights flash red across the board.

Here's the issue... The market is too giddy about the AI revolution. Nvidia gets a "D" grade from the Wall Street Truth Detector, and for good reason...

Nvidia's chips are essential to building AI systems. However, the company has also been a big beneficiary of crypto mining and data center booms for the past few years. And both of those booms have cooled off significantly.
